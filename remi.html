Remi: The Refiner’s Quest

The Bottleneck

The "Faith-Based Game" segment suffers from a binary failure: either dry, instructional content that ignores entertainment value, or hollow secular-clones that ignore spiritual substance. There is a lack of high-integrity, immersive environments designed to facilitate genuine spiritual formation.

The Architectural Solution

I am currently architecting a modular 2D RPG framework from the ground up in VS Code. The engine core leverages a recursive game-loop and Euclidean coordinate-based state management to handle complex quest-branching. I am utilizing a "Vibe Coding" methodology—a rapid-iteration, high-agency approach to development—to prototype these mechanics. The project serves as an active R&D playground, prioritizing narrative-driven spiritual discovery over the standard, grind-heavy loops common in the current market.
