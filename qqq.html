The Problem

In Global Support, the biggest bottleneck isn't the volume of cases—it's prioritization blindness. Consultants are flooded with information, but they lack a structured way to determine what actually matters versus what is just noise. Previous tools relied on static, hardcoded spreadsheets that couldn't adapt to real-time business needs.

The Solution

I built an AI-enabled prioritization engine that transforms static documentation into interactive, guided decision flows. Instead of forcing consultants to hunt for the right process, the engine surfaces the correct next action, ensuring 100% adherence to business logic.

  • Self-Serve Operations: I decoupled the decision logic from the content, meaning internal teams can update their own scenarios directly—no engineering help needed.
  • Data-Driven Outcomes: The system doesn't just "teach"; it measures. It tracks decision accuracy so leadership can see exactly how support behavior improves over time.
  • Infrastructure, Not Just a Prototype: While it started as a simulation, I architected this into production-ready software using a scalable PostgreSQL and TypeScript stack designed for enterprise-wide audit-readiness.
