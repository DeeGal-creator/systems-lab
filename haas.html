HaaS (Human-as-a-Service) Orchestration Engine

The Bottleneck

The fundamental failure in knowledge work isn't just volume—it's "prioritization blindness." Teams are chronically unable to distinguish between noise and critical-path items, leading to constant reactive triage, poor business alignment, and systemic burnout.

The Architectural Solution

I initially architected this as a high-velocity microsimulation game during a Hack Week to prove that prioritization logic could be gamified as a "sticky" learning tool. I later engineered the logic into a scalable, production-ready framework using Superblocks to handle the core engine state-management. The architecture treats human work as a prioritized data stream, mapping individual tasks to business-critical outcomes. This system is currently being refactored for independent deployment as a standalone decision-support tool.
