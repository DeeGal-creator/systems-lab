Devotional Intelligence Suite

The Bottleneck

Spiritual formation is often hindered by fragmented theological knowledge and the inability to maintain personalized continuity. Static devotionals fail to account for the user's current spiritual state, leaving them with "one-size-fits-all" content that doesn't map to their immediate life challenges or biblical needs.

The Architectural Solution

I am building an AI-orchestrated CMS that functions as an intelligent state-manager for spiritual growth. By capturing the user’s current state—emotional, circumstantial, and spiritual—the engine performs semantic-search queries across a validated scripture database (ESV/NASB). It dynamically synthesizes relevant truth-data into a personalized, actionable framework, ensuring that the user’s spiritual journey is grounded in consistent, contextually-relevant biblical application.

Command Stack

Supabase (pgvector) | LangGraph | Next.js | Semantic State-Mapping
